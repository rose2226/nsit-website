About NSIT

Empowering Communities Through Technology Education

Learn about our mission, vision, and commitment to bridging the digital divide in Uganda.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, quality computer skills training that empowers individuals in less advantaged communities to participate meaningfully in the digital economy and transform their lives through technology.

Our Vision

To be the leading community-focused technology education institute in Uganda, creating opportunities for economic empowerment and social transformation through accessible computer literacy programs.

Our Story

We are a Christian based organization that is focused on equipping computer skills to people in slam communities. Our lessons are completely free of charge and can be attended by anyone. We believe in a bright future, but it can only be bright if we work together on making it so. Our aim is to make sure our communities have the highly needed computer kills in order to be able to traverse this world that is turning digital on a fast pace.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at NSIT

Accessibility

We believe quality education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of economic background.

Practical Learning

Our courses focus on real-world skills that students can immediately apply in their careers.

Community Focus

We are deeply rooted in our community and committed to its growth and development.

Excellence

We maintain high standards in our teaching, facilities, and student support services.

Empowerment

We empower students with skills and confidence to succeed in the digital economy.

Innovation

We continuously update our curriculum to reflect current technology trends and market needs.
